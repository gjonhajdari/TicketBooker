Ticketbooker is an online platform for purchasing tickets to concerts,movies or flights internationally. Our services make it easy to book tickets from several booking websites and make them avalible in one place,in a single transaction,whether you are in the UK,the USA or Europe.No fees are charged at the point of purchase or after your event has ended.


TicketBooker operates in all EU countres,inceland,Norway and Switzerland.The founding team of TicketBooker was made up of entrepreneurs from London,Oslo and Pars.The company now employs 120 people. The service's technical platform has a back-end computer server with support of Citrix/Dell shared network servers and database.


In Septemver 2015,TicketBooker partnered with Worldline,one of Europe's leading provider of paymet servies, for TicketBooker's European ticket booking services,allowing the company to expant its persence to 2,500+ Live Nation & AXS corporate clients as well as 20,000+ independent music venues and promoters. TicketBooker has recieved $1.3m in funding from City Index Ventures in May 2014.
